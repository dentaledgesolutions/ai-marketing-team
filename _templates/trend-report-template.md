## Decision Thresholds

| Score | Decision |
|---|---|
| 85–100 | **Priority** — Use in next 7 days |
| 70–84 | **Meaningful** — Add to monthly content calendar |
| 50–69 | **Watchlist** — Monitor; do not produce yet |
| 0–49 | **Reject** |

**Hard veto — reject regardless of score if any of the following apply:**
- Compliance safety score ≤ 2
- Evidence quality score ≤ 2
- Trend encourages unsafe dental behavior or DIY treatment
- Trend involves diagnosis, self-treatment, or clinical claims without a provider
- Trend relies on shame-based or fear-based messaging
- Trend makes unverifiable before/after claims

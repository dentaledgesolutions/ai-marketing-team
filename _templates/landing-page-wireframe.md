## Conversion Optimization Notes

**Mobile-first checklist:**
- [ ] Primary CTA visible above the fold on a 375px mobile screen
- [ ] Phone CTA is a `tel:` link (tap to call)
- [ ] Form fields large enough to tap accurately on mobile
- [ ] Hero image does not slow page load (compressed, WebP format)
- [ ] No horizontal scrolling

**Ad traffic compliance (if paid traffic):**
- [ ] Meta: page does not use negative self-image language; no before/after patient photos
- [ ] Google: page content matches ad copy (no bait-and-switch)
- [ ] Both: page loads in under 3 seconds (Core Web Vitals)
